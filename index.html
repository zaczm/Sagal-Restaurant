About Sagal Restaurant

Welcome to Sagal Restaurant, where we bring authentic international flavors to the Twin Cities. Our restaurant was founded with a passion for creating memorable dining experiences through delicious food and warm hospitality.

Each dish is carefully prepared by our skilled chefs using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients whenever possible. We take pride in offering a diverse menu that caters to various tastes and dietary preferences.

Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, family dinner, or special celebration, we strive to make every visit to Sagal Restaurant exceptional.

Our Values
  • Quality ingredients in every dish
  • Authentic recipes from around the world
  • Warm, welcoming atmosphere
  • Commitment to customer satisfaction
